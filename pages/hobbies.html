Sports

I am a highly disciplined individual with a strong background in sports and teamwork. Throughout my life, I have practiced more than ten different disciplines, with my most significant experiences in football, volleyball, boxing, taekwondo, and baseball. During my time living in Mongolia, I had the opportunity to represent my high school in an international tournament in China, where we achieved second place. In my senior year, I was honored to serve as team captain, further strengthening my leadership and responsibility skills. In boxing, I have participated in numerous amateur fights and was part of my state league, gaining valuable experience in competition and resilience. I also hold a yellow belt in taekwondo, reflecting my commitment to discipline and continuous improvement. Additionally, I was selected to compete in the prestigious Copa Pony Malta as a member of my baseball team, an experience that reinforced my dedication, teamwork, and competitive spirit.

Charity

Giving back to the community has been an important part of my life since childhood. From a young age, I was actively involved in the Lions Club, where I participated in various initiatives such as tree planting, beach clean-ups, and donating essential materials to children and communities in need. Throughout my journey, I progressed through different stages of the organization, serving as a Cub and later as a Leo. Each corresponding to different age groups and levels of responsibility. During this time, I was also elected as the fiscal of my group, a role I held for one year before moving abroad, where I was responsible for managing and overseeing financial activities. These experiences shaped my sense of responsibility, empathy, and leadership. Looking ahead, once I achieve financial stability, I aspire to continue this path of service by becoming a Lion and further contributing to meaningful social impact.

Music

Music has always been an essential part of my life, as I come from a family of musicians who inspired my passion from an early age. Since childhood, I have played the guitar and later taught myself the basics of piano, developing my skills through curiosity and dedication. During my four years living in Germany, I also discovered a deep passion for DJing, which has become an important creative outlet for me. It allows me to explore sound, rhythm, and audience connection in a dynamic and expressive way. Combining my interest in music with my engineering mindset, I designed and 3D printed my own ocarina, which I use to play some of my favorite childhood songs. Music, for me, is both a creative pursuit and a way to stay connected to my roots.
