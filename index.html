About Me

My name is Angello Parody, and I am a Mechatronics Engineer who graduated from SRH Hochschule Berlin. I am currently pursuing my Master’s degree in AI Engineering of Autonomous Systems at Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt (THI), where I am focusing on the integration of artificial intelligence and robotics to develop intelligent, adaptive, and autonomous technologies.

Originally from Colombia, I’ve had the opportunity to live in several countries, including Guyana, Mongolia, and now Germany. Growing up in different cultural environments taught me adaptability, open-mindedness, and the value of connecting with people from diverse backgrounds. These experiences have shaped not only my global perspective but also my approach to teamwork and innovation.

I’m passionate about sports, music, and helping others—values that keep me grounded and motivated to contribute positively to society. I strongly believe that technology should serve humanity, and my goal is to work in a leading tech company where I can use my skills in robotics and AI to make a meaningful impact on the world.

Driven by curiosity and creativity, I aspire to bridge the gap between engineering and intelligent systems, developing technologies that improve everyday life and push the boundaries of what autonomous systems can achieve.
